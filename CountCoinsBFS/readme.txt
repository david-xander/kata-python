There are four types of common coins in US currency:
  o) quarters (25 cents)
  o) dimes (10 cents)
  o) nickels (5 cents)
  o) pennies (1 cent)

There are 6 ways to make change for 15 cents:
  o) A dime and a nickel; X
  o) A dime and 5 pennies; X
  o) 3 nickels; X
  o) 2 nickels and 5 pennies; X
  o) A nickel and 10 pennies; X
  o) 15 pennies. X

How many ways are there to make change for a dollar using these common coins? (1 dollar = 100 cents).
